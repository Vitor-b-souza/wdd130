Important Information

What to Bring

  • Swimsuit and towel
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Water shoes or sandals with straps
  • Change of clothes
  • Water bottle

What We Provide

  • Life jackets and helmets
  • Paddles and rafts
  • Experienced guides
  • Safety equipment
  • Waterproof bags

Safety Requirements

  • All participants must be able to swim
  • Follow guide instructions at all times
  • No alcohol before or during trips
  • Weight limit: 260 lbs per person
  • Valid waiver required for all participants
